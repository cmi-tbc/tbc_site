The Burnout Self-Assessment

Time to get clear on what's really going on.

This 15-question, research-backed self-assessment provides insight into your current burnout risk across five core domains.
Answer openly - there are no right or wrong answers and your responses remain confidential.
For each question, select the option that best reflects your experience over the past few weeks.

Domain 1: Exhaustion (Mental, Physical, Emotional)

1. I feel like I'm running on fumes, no matter how much I rest.





2. My brain feels foggy or sluggish, especially under pressure.





3. I go through the motions, but feel emotionally flat or depleted.





Domain 2: Disconnection (Withdrawal, Numbness, Cynicism)

4. I feel emotionally numb or disconnected from the people around me.





5. I avoid social activities because I just don't have the energy.





6. I care less and less about things I used to be passionate about.





Domain 3: Self-Efficacy Loss (Doubt, Shame, Defeat)

7. I question whether I'm still good at what I do.





8. I feel like everything I do is subpar or barely good enough.





9. I've lost faith in my ability to turn things around.





Domain 4: Overcompensation & Control (Guilt, Overfunctioning)

10. I feel guilty when I'm not being productive.





11. I say yes when I want to say no, just to avoid disappointing people.





12. I avoid asking for help because I don't want to seem weak.





Domain 5: Systemic Pressure & Misalignment (Environment & Values Conflict)

13. My workload is unrealistic for one person.





14. My values conflict with what my job demands.





15. I feel like the system is broken & I'm paying the price.
