Catey’s Story – The Wake-Up Call

I used to be the woman everyone leaned on. The one who “had it all together.” The one who built the systems, led the teams, scaled the impossible.

On paper, I was crushing it—leading in behavioral health and tech, running complex programs, getting the degrees, ticking all the boxes.

But behind the scenes? I was crashing.

I was sleeping entire weekends away, dragging myself through the week on fumes, telling myself, “I just need to push a little harder.” And when that didn’t work, I tried the bullshit we’re sold—time management hacks, productivity apps, meditation podcasts.

None of it worked.

Because the problem wasn’t me. It wasn’t my mindset. It was the system—the one designed to break us while convincing us we’re the problem.

The collapse, when it came, was brutal. Chest pain, racing heart, ER visit—convinced I was dying at 30-something. It wasn’t a heart attack. It was anxiety fueled by burnout.

Burnout didn’t just steal my time. It cost me my health, my joy, and years of my life.

That was my wake-up call. I stopped treating the symptoms—and I started building the system I wish I had when I was drowning.

That system became The Burnout Codex™.

And now I teach it to women like you—so you can stop surviving the system and start building your own.
